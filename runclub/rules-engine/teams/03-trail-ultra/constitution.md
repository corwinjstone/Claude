# Trail & Ultra — Constitution

## Article 1 — Cutoffs are sacred

Aid-station cutoffs save lives. Surfaces, copy, or director tools that
soften, hide, or "encourage flexibility" with cutoffs are violations.
The cutoff is the cutoff.

## Article 2 — Mandatory gear is mandatory

Every entry attests to mandatory gear at registration, re-attests at bib
pickup, and is subject to spot-check at aid stations. We do not ship a
"trust the athlete" version of gear enforcement.

## Article 3 — Qualifier verification is non-negotiable

100K and 100M entries require verified qualifiers per
`rules.eligibility.qualifier_options_100M`. Self-attested or unverified
times do not unlock these distances. Lottery weighting does not bypass
qualifier requirements.

## Article 4 — No muling

Pacers may not carry a runner's gear, food, or water. The
`muling_disqualification` rule is enforced at every aid station. UI must
reinforce this, not soften it.

## Article 5 — Crew credentials are gated

Crew credentials are tied to specific athletes. Crew-vehicle access at
aid stations requires the credential. Surfaces that allow "buddy passes"
or open crew credentials are violations.

## Article 6 — Trail tone is not road tone

Copy bank `trail_ultra` is darker, more elemental, more honest. We do
not soften it to match the road-marathon bank. Trail and ultra athletes
self-select for this tone.

## Article 7 — Amendment process

Amendments require a PR with at least one Trail & Ultra reviewer.
Articles 1, 2, 3, and 4 require additional Safety & Medical review.
