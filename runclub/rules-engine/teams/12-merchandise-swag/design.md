# Merchandise & Swag — Design Rules

Inherits global Design System.

## Race shirts

- Front: race name in Futura Condensed bold italic UPPER, distance hero
  numeric in `display-xl` italic.
- Back: division copy-bank tagline in Futura Condensed bold italic
  UPPER. No marketing copy, no sponsor logos that violate the
  Sponsorship & Partnerships rules.
- Color: black or white shirt body. Volt accent allowed for screen
  print. Ember reserved for trail-ultra and OCR shirts.

## Medals

- Distance hero numeric on the medal face: `100`, `26.2`, `13.1`, etc.
- Race name in Futura Condensed bold italic UPPER ribbon.
- No personalized medals. Personalization is an e-commerce feature; we
  are not e-commerce.

## Bibs

- Bib number in `display-mega` italic tabular, centered.
- Athlete first name in `headline-l` italic UPPER above. Last name not
  printed (privacy).
- Sponsor accent strip across the bottom; sponsor logos per
  Sponsorship & Partnerships brand kit.

## Packet-pickup surfaces

- Operational density. Manifest tables, tabular figures.
- "READY FOR PICKUP" pill in `volt`. "ALREADY PICKED UP" pill in `mist`.

## Copy

Athlete-facing merch copy uses the division's copy bank. We do not
ship cute apparel descriptions ("buttery soft tri-blend, perfect for
post-run brunch"). The shirt is a finisher shirt, not a lifestyle item.
