# Merchandise & Swag — Constitution

## Article 1 — Race merch, not lifestyle merch

We sell race-issued shirts, medals, finisher items, and event-specific
merchandise. We do not run a general apparel store, lifestyle brand,
or year-round merch shop. RunClub is not an e-commerce platform.

## Article 2 — Sizing is captured at registration

Shirt size and other sized items are captured per
`rules.registration_required_fields` at the registration step that
creates the entry. We do not ship "select sizing later" flows that
fragment the manifest.

## Article 3 — Medals ship after finish, not after registration

Finisher medals are earned. Surfaces that ship a medal pre-race or to
a non-finisher are violations. Virtual races are the documented
exception per `rules.for_division("virtual").pricing`.

## Article 4 — Inventory matches registration, not aspirations

Print/manufacture quantities match registration counts plus a
documented buffer percentage. We do not order to forecast; we order
to manifest.

## Article 5 — Amendment process

Amendments require a PR with at least one Merchandise & Swag reviewer.
