# Volunteer Coordination — Design Rules

Inherits global Design System.

## Volunteer surfaces are quieter than athlete surfaces

Volunteer flows are operational. The motivational copy bank is
`volunteer` — encouraging but not race-energy.

## Hero numerics

- Shift count, hours committed, and lottery-credit hours render as
  `display-l` italic numerics ("8 HOURS / 1 LOTTERY CREDIT").
- Role headcounts on the director side render as `display-l` italic
  ("FINISH LINE: 22 / 30 FILLED").

## Background-check status

`volt` "CLEARED" pill, `mist` "PENDING" pill, `ember` "EXPIRED" pill.
Pills are always visible on the volunteer's profile and on every shift
they're slotted to.

## Copy bank

Mandatory: `volunteer`. Examples:
- "RACES DON'T HAPPEN WITHOUT YOU."
- "EIGHT HOURS. ONE BIB CREDIT."
- "STAND ON THE OTHER SIDE OF THE FINISH LINE."
