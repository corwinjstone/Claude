# Volunteer Coordination — Constitution

## Article 1 — Volunteers are not athletes

Volunteer profiles and athlete profiles are separate identities.
A human may have both, but the surfaces, communications, and waivers
are distinct.

## Article 2 — Background-check requirements are gated

Volunteer roles requiring background checks (medical, finish-line,
chip-collection, course-marshal lead) are gated by check status. We do
not ship "approved provisionally" paths.

## Article 3 — Shift-swap requires re-attestation

A volunteer who swaps into a role re-attests to that role's
requirements (training modules, waivers, dress code) before the
swap is effective.

## Article 4 — Volunteers are credited

Volunteers earn lottery-weighting credit per
`rules.lottery.weighted_entries_for_volunteering_8_hours` where the
division supports it. We do not ship volunteer flows that fail to
record this credit.

## Article 5 — Amendment process

Amendments require a PR with at least one Volunteer Coordination
reviewer.
