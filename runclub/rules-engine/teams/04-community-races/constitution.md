# Community Races — Constitution

These articles are non-negotiable for any surface owned by this team.
Amendments require a PR against this file with Community Races team
approval.

## Article 1 — First-timer first

Every flow is designed for a person who has never raced before. If a
choice trades simplicity for power-user features, simplicity wins. The
runner who has done 14 marathons can figure out the simple flow; the
runner registering for their first 5K cannot figure out the complex one.

## Article 2 — No jargon

Banned words on any community surface: **corral, PR, negative split, BQ,
fartlek, chip time vs. gun time, age-graded, peloton, pacing strategy,
AG award, taper, carb-load.** If a concept must appear, it must be
explained in one sentence using everyday language.

## Article 3 — One-page registration

The default community registration is one page, one scroll. Multi-page
flows, expand/collapse sections, and "advanced options" toggles are
violations. If a field is not required to put a person on a start line,
it does not appear.

## Article 4 — Cash and walk-up are first-class

A meaningful fraction of community athletes register with cash on race
morning. Surfaces that assume credit-card-only or pre-registration-only
are violations. The walk-up iPad flow is held to the same quality bar as
the web flow.

## Article 5 — Welcoming, not intimidating

The Design System's typography stays bold and italic — that's not
negotiable. But the **copy bank** for community is `community_first_timer`:
encouraging, not aggressive. "EVERY FINISHER STARTED WITH ONE." not
"THE TRAIL DOESN'T NEGOTIATE." Race the right energy at the right
audience.

## Article 6 — Family-bundle is a feature, not a discount line

A parent registering a kid for the dash is the modal community
transaction. Family bundle UI is a primary path, not a checkbox in
checkout.

## Article 7 — Amendment process

Amendments require a PR against this file with at least one Community
Races reviewer approval. AI agents may not approve PRs against this file.
