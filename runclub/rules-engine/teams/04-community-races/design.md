# Community Races — Design Rules

These rules layer on top of the global Design System. Where they are
stricter, they win. Where the global system is stricter, it wins.

## 1. Type system

- Use the Design System's bold-italic Futura Condensed hierarchy. Do not
  request a "softer" or "calmer" variant — the system stays loud.
- Distance hero (`5K`, `10K`) renders in `display-xl` italic minimum.
- "REGISTER" CTA renders in `headline-l` italic UPPER on a `volt` button.

## 2. Color

- Default accent: `volt`.
- Family-bundle and kids'-dash modules may use `chalk` on `ink` with
  volt accents. No additional accents.
- **Banned for community surfaces:** `ember`. Community races do not have
  the urgency framing that ember signals; reserve it for trail/ultra,
  OCR, and race-day medical surfaces.

## 3. Copy bank

- Mandatory copy bank: `community_first_timer`.
- Sample lines (curated, not generated):
  - "EVERY FINISHER STARTED WITH ONE."
  - "YOUR FIRST 5K STARTS HERE."
  - "GO AT YOUR PACE. JUST GO."
  - "THE START LINE IS FOR EVERYONE."
- Forbidden lines on community surfaces:
  - "THE TRAIL DOESN'T NEGOTIATE." (trail-ultra bank)
  - "26.2 MILES. NO EXCUSES." (marathon bank)
  - Any line invoking elite-tier intensity.

## 4. Imagery

- Required: smiling finishers, family/kids, walkers, strollers, leashed
  dogs, costume-themed events. Community surfaces are the one place where
  finish-line *joy* outranks finish-line *grit*.
- Still forbidden: gear flat-lays, stock photography, wellness aesthetics.

## 5. Component patterns

- Walk-up iPad flow uses the same component library as the web flow at
  touch-friendly sizes. No "lite" variant.
- Family-bundle component renders `6` in `display-xl` italic ("UP TO 6
  ATHLETES"), not as a fine-print disclaimer.
- Kids'-dash component is full-bleed on its surface — kids racing is a
  hero feature, not a buried option.

## 6. Mobile-first

80% of community registrations come from a phone. Layouts are designed
mobile-first; desktop is the wider variant, not the canonical one.
