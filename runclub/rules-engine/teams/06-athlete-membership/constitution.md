# Athlete Membership — Constitution

## Article 1 — One athlete, one profile

A human has one RunClub profile. We do not ship multi-account flows,
"alt" profiles, or "race-day-only" temporary profiles. Identity is
canonical.

## Article 2 — Verified ≠ self-attested

The PR registry separates verified finishes (RunClub-timed events) from
self-attested ones. Surfaces that display PRs must always render the
verification status. We do not allow "trust-me" PRs to be displayed
identically to verified ones.

## Article 3 — RunClub is not a training app

We read Strava and Garmin connections for **verified race results
only**. We do not ingest workouts, weekly mileage, sleep, HRV, or any
training-adjacent data. This is a hard product line.

## Article 4 — Privacy by default

Athlete profile data is private by default. Race results are public per
the event's settings. Athlete-controlled toggles do not bury the privacy
default behind dark patterns.

## Article 5 — Youth profiles require guardian linkage

Profiles for athletes under 18 require a verified guardian link. We do
not ship "claim later" flows that allow a youth profile to exist
unsupervised.

## Article 6 — Amendment process

Amendments require a PR with at least one Athlete Membership reviewer.
Articles 3 and 4 require additional product-leadership sign-off.
