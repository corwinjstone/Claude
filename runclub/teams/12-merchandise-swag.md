# Team: Merchandise & Race Swag

## Mission
Run the race-merch line: shirts, medals, bibs, finisher gear, expo
merch. Critically, this is *race-attached* merchandise — RunClub does
not run a general-purpose apparel store.

## Scope
- Race-shirt sizing and ordering at registration (cotton/tech split,
  unisex/women's-cut, sizing chart)
- Finisher medals and finisher-gear add-ons
- Bib design and printing (with sponsor logos from Sponsorship team)
- Packet-pickup logistics (paired with Course & Logistics on
  pickup-location physical setup)
- Expo merch booth (race-branded gear sold day-of)
- Post-race merch storefront (open for ~30 days after race for late
  buyers and out-of-stock-on-race-day athletes)
- Inventory forecasting from registration trends

## Out of Scope
- General athletic apparel sales unrelated to a specific race
- Training gear, shoes, electronics, nutrition products (RunClub does
  not sell these)

## Key Workflows
1. **Sizing capture** — at registration, athlete picks size; lock-in
   date set per event (typically 30 days out, after which size changes
   become "best effort").
2. **Ordering window** — director sets a quantity and 5%-overage policy;
   system fires PO to vendor at lock-in.
3. **Packet-pickup roster** — Course & Logistics places pickup tables;
   we generate per-table athlete manifests.
4. **Post-race storefront** — leftover inventory sold for 30 days,
   revenue split with director per contract.

## Dependencies
- All registration teams (sizing data captured in their flows)
- Sponsorship (logo files for bibs, co-branded shirts)
- Course & Logistics (packet-pickup physical setup)
