# Team: Sponsorship & Partnerships

## Mission
Connect race directors with sponsors, and connect RunClub with national
brand partners. Sponsors fund the races; we make the inventory (banner
spots, expo booths, branded miles, free-entry allocations) sellable in
a structured way.

## Scope
- Sponsor inventory definition per event (title, presenting, gold,
  silver, expo, mile-marker, aid-station-naming)
- Sponsor activation tracking (logos placed, banners delivered, comp
  entries issued)
- Free-entry allocation: sponsors get N comp entries, distributable via
  one-time codes that flow back through registration teams
- Expo booth registration (vendors selling at packet pickup)
- National partnerships (e.g., a hydration brand sponsoring all RunClub
  trail ultras)
- Charity partner program (charity orgs get a charity-bib allocation)

## Out of Scope
- Athlete-facing merchandise sales (Merchandise & Swag owns)
- Sponsor logo placement on race-day signage manufacturing (race director
  responsibility)

## Key Workflows
1. **Sponsor package builder** — director defines tiers and inventory;
   system generates sales-ready PDF + Stripe invoice link.
2. **Comp-entry distribution** — sponsor receives a code; sponsor's
   employees / customers redeem at registration.
3. **Activation checklist** — race week, system pings sponsor and
   director to confirm logo files received, banner shipped, etc.

## Dependencies
- Race Director Services (sponsor contracts visible in director dashboard)
- All registration teams (comp codes apply at checkout)
- Merchandise & Swag (co-branded swag opportunities)
