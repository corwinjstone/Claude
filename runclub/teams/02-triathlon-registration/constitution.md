# Triathlon Registration — Constitution

## Article 1 — Three sports, one entry

Triathlon registration captures swim, bike, and run data in a single
flow. We do not split the entry into three separate registrations or
"add-on" flows. The athlete enters one race, with three legs.

## Article 2 — USAT membership is mandatory

Every triathlon entry verifies an active USAT membership or sells a
one-day license at checkout. We do not allow "I'll provide it later"
flows for sanctioned events.

## Article 3 — Bike data is safety data

`bike_make_model` is not a marketing field. It feeds transition-rack
manifests and crash-investigation records. Surfaces that present it as
optional, fun, or social are violations.

## Article 4 — Wetsuit declaration is binding

The athlete's wetsuit declaration determines wave start, transition rack
location, and (in some events) eligibility. We do not allow silent
changes after registration close.

## Article 5 — 70.3 / 140.6 entries require physician sign-off

Per `rules.medical.physician_signoff_required_for_full`, full-distance
entries require physician documentation. We do not ship a "skip the
doctor" path. Charity, comp, or any other entry path still requires it.

## Article 6 — Amendment process

Amendments require a PR with at least one Triathlon Registration
reviewer. AI agents may not approve PRs against this file.
