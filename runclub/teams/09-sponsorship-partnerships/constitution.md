# Sponsorship & Partnerships — Constitution

## Article 1 — Sponsors live on marketing surfaces, not registration

Sponsor logos, links, and copy belong on marketing pages, race-page
hero modules, and post-finish share cards. They do not appear inside
the registration funnel between "start" and "confirmation."

## Article 2 — The design system is the brand floor

Sponsor brand kits extend the RunClub Design System; they do not
replace it. A co-branded surface still uses Futura Condensed bold
italic, still uses RunClub tokens, still uses the motivational copy
banks. Sponsor color may appear as an additional accent, never as the
primary type or surface color.

## Article 3 — Athlete data is not for sale

Sponsor partnerships do not include access to athlete PII, contact
information, or behavior data. Co-branded campaigns reach athletes via
RunClub-controlled channels with athlete-controlled opt-in.

## Article 4 — Sanctioning bodies are partners, not sponsors

USATF, USAT, RRCA, ITRA, UTMB, and similar sanctioning bodies are
governed by separate agreements, not the sponsor framework. Their logos
appear where required by sanctioning rules and follow their own usage
guidelines.

## Article 5 — Amendment process

Amendments require a PR with at least one Sponsorship & Partnerships
reviewer. Article 3 requires additional product-leadership and legal
sign-off.
